French Toast

Description

If you are wondering how to make the tastiest french toast then you are about to get all your answers in the article below. This tasty and easy recipe requires only 5 ingredients that you would have already at hand.

Here's everything that you will need to make the tastiest french toast of your life:

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tea spoon vanilla extract(optional)
  • Â¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon (Optional)
  • salt to taste
  • thick slices bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, or more as needed

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  4. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  5. Serve hot.
