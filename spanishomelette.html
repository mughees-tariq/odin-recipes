Spanish Omelette

Description

If you are wondering how to make the tastiest Spanish Omelette then you are about to get all your answers in the article below. This tasty and easy recipe requires only 7 ingredients that you would have already at hand.

Here's everything that you will need to make the tastiest Spanish Omelette of your life:

Ingredients

  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ½ pound potatoes, thinly sliced
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 medium tomatoes - peeled, seeded, and coarsely chopped (Optional)
  • 2 green onions, chopped (Optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  4. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  5. Serve hot.
